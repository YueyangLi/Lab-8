Turing Test ============= The Turing Test is a test developed by Alan Turing in 1950. It was a test of a machine's ability to exhibit intellient behavior equivalent to, or indistinguishable form, that of a human. Alan Turing ============= Alan Turing is a researcher in the UK, who had been exploring "mahcine intelligence" for up to ten years.
